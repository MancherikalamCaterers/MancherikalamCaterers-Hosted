About Mancherikalam Caterers

Let us make your special occasions truly memorable.

Established in 1990, Mancherikalam Caterers has been a trusted name in catering services in Changanacherry, Kottayam. With over three decades of experience, we specialize in delivering exceptional catering solutions for weddings, corporate events, birthday parties, and more.

Our team of skilled chefs and professional staff ensures that every dish is prepared with high-quality ingredients, offering a diverse menu that caters to different tastes and preferences. Whether it’s a grand wedding feast or an intimate gathering, we provide customized catering packages tailored to meet your needs and budget.
