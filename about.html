About Mancherikalam Caterers

Serving Excellence Since 1990

Our Story

From a Passion for Cooking to a Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1990, Mancherikalam Caterers began as a humble family-run business with a passion for delivering exceptional culinary experiences. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most trusted catering services in Changanacherry, Kottayam, known for our delicious food, impeccable service, and attention to detail.

From small gatherings to grand weddings and corporate events, our journey has been shaped by a commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. With a team of expert chefs and professional staff, we ensure that every event we cater is a memorable one.

Why Choose Us

Delivering exceptional taste and service for over 35 years.

We offer customized menus, diverse cuisines, and professional service to make every event special. With a team of skilled chefs and a commitment to quality, we ensure a seamless and delightful catering experience.
